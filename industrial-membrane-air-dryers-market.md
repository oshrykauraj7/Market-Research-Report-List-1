<p><ul><li>Porous Type Industrial Membrane Air Dryers</li><li>Non-Porous Type Industrial Membrane Air Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial membrane air dryers market comprises two main types: porous and non-porous. Porous type industrial membrane air dryers utilize semi-permeable membranes that allow the passage of water vapor while retaining air, effectively dehumidifying it. Non-porous type industrial membrane air dryers, on the other hand, use membranes that do not permit moisture to pass, relying on pressure differentials to separate moisture from air. Both types are essential for enhancing air quality and protecting equipment in various industrial applications.</p></p>
