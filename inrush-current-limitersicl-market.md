<p><p>Inrush Current Limiters (ICL) are devices used to control high initial currents that can damage electrical components during startup. The market for ICLs primarily includes NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) inrush current limiters, which decrease resistance as they heat up, allowing safe current flow. PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) inrush current limiters increase resistance with temperature, effectively limiting current. Other types may include specialized circuitry or hybrid solutions. These devices are essential in various applications, ensuring system reliability and longevity.</p></p>
