<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Type</li><li>Capacitive Electronic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water descaler market features two primary types: electromagnetic and capacitive electronic. Electromagnetic descalers use magnetic fields to alter water's properties, reducing scale buildup in plumbing and appliances. They typically involve wrapping a coil around the pipe, allowing treated water to flow through. Capacitive electronic descalers, on the other hand, employ an electronic board to emit signals that affect scale formation without chemicals. Both types aim to improve water quality, enhance appliance longevity, and lower maintenance costs.</p></p>
