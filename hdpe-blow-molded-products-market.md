<p><p>The HDPE (high-density polyethylene) blow molded products market primarily focuses on two manufacturing methods: blow molding and injection molding. Blow molding involves inflating molten plastic into a mold, creating hollow shapes ideal for containers like bottles and tanks. In contrast, injection molding injects molten HDPE into rigid molds, producing solid parts with precise dimensions. Both methods cater to diverse applications across industries, emphasizing efficiency and scalability, with blow molding typically favored for lightweight, hollow products and injection molding for complex, durable items.</p></p>
