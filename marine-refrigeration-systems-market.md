<p><p>Marine refrigeration systems are essential for maintaining optimal temperatures on vessels. The market is primarily divided into two types: air cooling and water cooling. Air cooling systems utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them simpler and more cost-effective for smaller vessels. In contrast, water cooling systems leverage marine water for heat exchange, offering more efficient cooling, especially in larger vessels or harsh environments. Each type serves specific applications, balancing efficiency, installation costs, and operational needs in marine settings.</p></p>
