<p><strong>The Organic Food Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Soluble</li><li>Oil-Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic food color market is divided into two primary types: water-soluble and oil-soluble colors. Water-soluble colors, often derived from fruits, vegetables, or natural extracts, are used in beverages, dairy products, and confections, providing vibrant shades while dissolving easily in water-based formulations. In contrast, oil-soluble colors are suited for fatty or oily products like snacks, baked goods, and sauces, maintaining stability and vibrancy in oil-rich formulations. Both types cater to growing consumer demand for natural and clean-label products.</p></p>
