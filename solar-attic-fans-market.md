<p><p>The solar attic fans market features three primary types: gable-mounted, roof-mounted, and portable fans. Gable-mounted fans are installed on the vertical gable wall of the attic, providing efficient ventilation. Roof-mounted fans sit on the roof surface, drawing heat and moisture out from the attic space, ideal for various roof styles. Portable fans, on the other hand, are designed for mobility, allowing users to position them wherever ventilation is needed. Each type enhances energy efficiency and helps regulate attic temperatures.</p></p>
