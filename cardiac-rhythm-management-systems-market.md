<p><p>Cardiac Rhythm Management Systems encompass various devices for managing heart rhythms. HF Management Systems focus on heart failure monitoring and treatment. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices improve heart efficiency by coordinating contractions in patients with heart dysfunction. Implantable Cardiac Defibrillators (ICDs) detect and correct life-threatening arrhythmias, delivering shocks when necessary. Pacemakers regulate heartbeats in individuals with irregular rhythms, ensuring consistent heart function. Together, these technologies enhance patient outcomes and quality of life in managing cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
