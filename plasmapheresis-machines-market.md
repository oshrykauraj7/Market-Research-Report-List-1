Plasma collection systems are designed specifically for the extraction of plasma from blood, facilitating the collection process for donors. In contrast, multi-component collection systems can simultaneously extract various blood components, including plasma, platelets, and red blood cells, optimizing the collection process for healthcare providers. Both types play a crucial role in blood donation and transfusion services, enhancing the efficiency and quality of the collected components.</p></p>
