<p><p>The polypropylene filament market is segmented into two main types: polypropylene multifilament and polypropylene monofilament. Polypropylene multifilament consists of multiple strands woven together, offering flexibility, durability, and lightweight properties, often used in textiles, fishing nets, and ropes. In contrast, polypropylene monofilament consists of a single strand, known for its strength and rigidity, making it suitable for applications like sewing threads, medical devices, and industrial uses. Both types are valued for their resistance to chemicals, UV light, and moisture.</p></p>
