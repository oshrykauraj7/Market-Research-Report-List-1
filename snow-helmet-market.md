<p><ul><li>ABS Material</li><li>PC Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow helmet market is primarily segmented based on materials, notably ABS and PC (polycarbonate). ABS helmets are known for their durability and impact resistance, making them ideal for casual skiers and snowboarders. They tend to be more affordable and offer good protection. On the other hand, PC helmets are lighter and have superior shock absorption properties, catering to professional athletes and those seeking high-performance gear. Both materials serve distinct consumer needs while prioritizing safety and comfort in winter sports.</p></p>
