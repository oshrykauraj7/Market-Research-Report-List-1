<p><p>The skin replacement and substitute market consists of various types aimed at treating wounds and injuries. Allografts are skin tissues from donors, while xenografts use animal skin. Acellular dermal matrices support healing without living cells. Amniotic membranes promote regeneration and healing. Synthetic skin products offer alternative solutions mimicking natural skin properties.</p></p>
