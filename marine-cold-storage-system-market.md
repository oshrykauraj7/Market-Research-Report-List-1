<p><p>The Marine Cold Storage System market features two primary types: Dual Temperature Cold Storage and Single Temperature Cold Storage. Dual Temperature Cold Storage is designed to maintain different temperatures within the same facility, allowing for the storage of various products, such as fresh seafood and frozen items, optimizing space and efficiency. In contrast, Single Temperature Cold Storage maintains a consistent temperature throughout, ideal for products requiring uniform storage conditions, enhancing the preservation of specific types of seafood during transport and storage.</p></p>
