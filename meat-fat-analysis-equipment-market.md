<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meat fat analysis equipment market features various technologies aimed at assessing fat content in meat products. DEXA (Dual Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) technology provides precise measurements by scanning meat with different energy X-ray beams, effectively distinguishing between fat and lean tissues. NIR (Near-Infrared Reflectance) technology, on the other hand, uses infrared light to analyze the chemical composition of meat, enabling quick and non-destructive fat content assessment. Both technologies enhance quality control and compliance in meat production and processing.</p></p>
