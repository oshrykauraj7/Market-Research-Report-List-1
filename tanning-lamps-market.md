<p><p>The tanning lamps market is divided into two primary types: low pressure and high pressure. Low pressure tanning lamps utilize fluorescent bulbs that emit UV light, providing a more even tan over a longer session. They are typically safer, producing less UVB radiation. Conversely, high pressure tanning lamps use quartz bulbs and emit a higher intensity of UVA light, allowing for faster tanning sessions but with increased risk. Each type caters to different consumer preferences regarding tanning speed and safety.</p></p>
