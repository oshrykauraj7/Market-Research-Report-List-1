<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Turntables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-drive Turntable</li><li>Belt-drive Turntable</li><li>Idler-wheel Turntable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turntable market comprises three main types: direct-drive, belt-drive, and idler-wheel turntables. Direct-drive turntables feature a motor directly connected to the platter, offering quick start-up and consistent speed, ideal for DJs. Belt-drive turntables use a rubber belt to isolate motor vibrations, providing superior sound quality, making them popular among audiophiles. Idler-wheel turntables utilize a rubber wheel to connect the motor to the platter, known for their vintage appeal and robust performance, appealing to collectors and enthusiasts.</p></p>
