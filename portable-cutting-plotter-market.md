<p><p>The portable cutting plotter market can be segmented into three main types: roll-to-roll, flatbed (UV), and others. Roll-to-roll plotters are designed for continuous materials, allowing for large-scale vinyl and banner production. Flatbed (UV) cutters cater to rigid substrates, facilitating precise cuts on various materials like plastics and wood while using UV printing technology. Other types may include hybrid models and specialty cutters optimized for unique applications, catering to diverse industries such as signage, graphics, and packaging.</p></p>
